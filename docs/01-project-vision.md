# Court Booking Platform

## Overview

The Court Booking Platform is a web application that connects sports venue owners with players looking to reserve courts.

Venue owners can register their venues, manage courts, and configure availability. Players can search available venues and make bookings.

The platform is initially focused on racket sports such as badminton and pickleball, but is designed to support additional sports in the future.

---

## Users

### Players

Players can:
- Register an account
- Search venues
- View available courts
- Make bookings
- Manage their bookings


### Venue Owners

Venue owners can:
- Register venues
- Manage courts
- Configure availability
- View bookings


### Platform Administrators

Administrators can:
- Manage users
- Manage venues
- Oversee platform activity
